Pixels Fund

How does it work?

  • Savvy investors choose Pixels Fund to manage their EXP endowment.
  • During the competition, these investors relax, rest assured that their funds are being invested wisely.
  • Before the end of the competition our crack squad of financial geniuses invests the fund optimally.
  • After the funds are distributed, Pixels Fund will distribute earnings out to our investors.

How do I invest?

  1. Go to your wallet
  2. Enter your Private Key
  3. Click Unlock
  4. Click here to copy our address
  5. Enter the Amount
  6. Enter Gas Limit
  7. Click Generate Transaction
  8. Scroll down
  9. Click Send Transaction

How do earnings work?

  • We wrote a smart contract that registers investors' deposits and automatically splits any dividends to investors after the winners are announced.
  • Dividends are distributed based on participation in the fund, using the following formula: (Investor's Participation/Total Fund) * Total Earnings
  • A small fee will then be charged prior to distribution, resulting in a 70/30 split, in which investors receive 70% and the fund keeps 30%.
